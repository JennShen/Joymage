Search item by image

Whether it is a snap of a photo or a saved photo, the app will find the fashion item along with similar products. You are prompted to choose a category that the item belongs in in order to refine the search results. Underneath the Camera and Photo buttons, you can see what trending items other Joymage users are searching.

Discover and customize items

The Explore tab shows popular products from various retailers. You can customize the results by selecting the items by price, discount, time, and color.

    • Favorite items

    Once you see an item that is of your liking, you can tap the heart icon to save the item to your Favorites list. Each item's product page includes a link to its website for purchase.
